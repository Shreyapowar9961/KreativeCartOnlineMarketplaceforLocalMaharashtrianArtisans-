PRODUCT DETAILS


The wooden damru is a traditional percussion instrument, shaped like an hourglass and crafted from high-quality wood. Known for its unique rhythmic sound, it is often associated with Lord Shiva. The instrument is made with animal skin stretched over its surface, producing a distinct beat when played. It’s both a functional musical tool and a beautifully crafted piece.
